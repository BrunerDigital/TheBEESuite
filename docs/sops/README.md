# The BEE Suite Role SOP Library

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Use these SOPs when training a school team or sending role-specific instructions before a launch, pilot, or parent portal rollout.

## Send-Out Order

1. Directors and assistant directors: send `DIRECTOR_SOP.md` first. Directors need to confirm families, classrooms, teachers, billing, documents, and parent access before the other role guides are useful.
2. Teachers: send `TEACHER_SOP.md` after directors confirm teacher accounts, classroom assignments, and roster accuracy.
3. Parents and guardians: send `PARENT_PORTAL_SOP.md` only after guardian emails are correct on the family profile and the school is ready for parent portal use.

## Role Guides

- `docs/sops/DIRECTOR_SOP.md` - director daily operations, billing, parent portal launch, documents, communications, and escalation.
- `docs/sops/TEACHER_SOP.md` - classroom attendance, daily reports, media, incidents, messages, staff kiosk, and offline queue behavior.
- `docs/sops/PARENT_PORTAL_SOP.md` - parent login, family dashboard, invoices, ACH/card payment, documents, messages, incident acknowledgements, and troubleshooting.

## Training Notes

- Keep each training session role-specific. Do not train parents on director or teacher workflows.
- Use real school examples only when the data has already been reviewed for accuracy.
- Do not share admin screenshots with parents if the screenshot includes another family, child, balance, staff record, incident, or document.
- Treat custody, medical, billing, and incident information as need-to-know.
- If a workflow looks wrong during training, stop and fix the underlying record before telling staff to work around it.

## Launch Sign-Off

Before the guides are sent broadly, the school should confirm:

- Directors can log in and see the correct school.
- Teachers can log in and see the correct classroom rosters.
- Parent guardian emails are accurate on family profiles.
- Parent portal login is available from `https://thebeesuite.io/login`.
- The default parent password is `BusyBees` unless a parent has already reset their password.
- Stripe payout and checkout readiness are complete before parents are asked to pay online.
- Billing and document workflows have been tested with one low-risk family record.
- The support escalation path is clear for directors, teachers, and parents.
