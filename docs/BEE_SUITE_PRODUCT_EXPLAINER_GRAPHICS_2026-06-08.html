The Daily Operating Loop

The BEE Suite is built around the actual rhythm of a childcare center: morning readiness, drop-off, classroom documentation, parent communication, billing, compliance, and executive follow-up.

Morning

Command check

Directors review today's tours, staffing, ratios, messages, birthdays, incidents, and licensing reminders.

  • Center dashboard
  • Notifications
  • AI summary
Drop-off

Attendance and ratios

Families and staff check in, classrooms fill, and leadership watches ratio and late arrival signals.

  • Check-in/out
  • Classroom roster
  • Pickup verification
Classroom day

Teacher logs

Teachers record meals, naps, bottles, diapers, activities, notes, media, and incident reports.

  • Daily reports
  • Medication logs
  • Incident creation
Family loop

Parent communication

Parents receive updates, messages, documents, invoices, acknowledgments, and notification choices.

  • Threaded messages
  • Parent portal
  • Email/SMS/push
Closeout

Reports and follow-up

Leadership reviews billing, compliance, FTE, incidents, overdue tasks, and multi-location rollups.

  • FTE reports
  • Billing status
  • Executive rollup
