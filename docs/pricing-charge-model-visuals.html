The Bee Suite
Pricing model visuals for location-based SaaS revenue

Ways to charge each childcare location.

A team review board for monthly platform revenue, payment processing participation, franchise rollups, onboarding fees, and optional usage-based add-ons.

Decision lens

Keep the core CRM easy to buy, then let payments, automations, communications, and enterprise reporting expand ARPU as schools mature.

Model 1

Flat monthly per location

$149-$499

Simple SaaS invoice for each school. Best for fast sales and predictable MRR.

  • CRM, leads, tours, embed form
  • Basic reporting and support
  • Best v1 anchor model
Model 2

Base + enrolled child band

$199 + tier

Location fee scales with school size, capacity, or active children.

  • Fairer for small centers
  • Captures value from larger sites
  • Needs clean child counts
Model 3

Payments platform fee

25-75 bps

When parent tuition flows through Stripe Connect, Bee Suite keeps a configurable platform fee.

  • High upside with tuition volume
  • Must disclose clearly
  • Can cap by location
Model 4

Brand or franchise license

Custom

HQ pays for the network, with location usage tracked underneath.

  • Best for Kid City style groups
  • Central admin and reporting fee
  • Volume discounts by center count
Model 5

Add-on marketplace

Usage

Optional paid modules increase ARPU without making the entry package heavy.

  • SMS, AI, e-sign, storage
  • FTE and executive reporting
  • Integrations and automations
Recommended V1 bundle

Hybrid: base SaaS + payment fee + add-ons

Launch with a clear per-location subscription, add a small configurable platform fee only when payments are processed through Bee Suite, and keep premium modules optional.

What to avoid early

  • Charging only on payment volume before parent billing is live.
  • Making every feature usage-based; directors need predictable bills.
  • Blending Stripe processing fees and Bee Suite platform fees in a way schools cannot audit.
