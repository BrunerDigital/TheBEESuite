Teacher / classroom staff

Teacher Dashboard

Touch-friendly classroom workflow for daily operations, attendance, reports, incidents, media, and parent updates.

Classroom Tabs

  • Teacher Portal, Classroom Dashboard, Attendance, Daily Reports, Incident Reports, Messages, Notifications, and Help.
  • Roster filter, child cards, room transfers, check-in/out, supplies, staff notes, and quick log buttons.

Daily Child Logs

  • Meals, bottles, naps, diapers, potty, activities, mood, learning notes, supplies, observations, and parent-facing daily reports.
  • Photo/video upload workflow routed through permission-aware director review before parent sharing.

Safety and Incidents

  • Incident creation with child, room, staff, type, description, action taken, attachments placeholder, and director review.
  • Medication log visibility and restricted child details only where the assigned role is allowed.

Communication

  • Parent/classroom conversations, per-family reply history, template-based replies, staff assignment, and notification preferences.
  • AI can polish notes and wording, but teacher submissions remain human-reviewed for sensitive workflows.
Parent / guardian

Parent Portal

Own-family dashboard for messages, child updates, documents, balances, payments, incidents, and preferences.

Family Tabs

  • Parent Portal, Messages, Documents, Billing & Invoices, Payments, Notifications, and Help.
  • Child profile summary, announcements, calendar items, daily reports, forms, and school contact options.

Child Updates

  • Daily reports, meals, naps, activities, teacher notes, photos/videos after approval, incident notices, and parent acknowledgments.
  • Emergency contact and authorized pickup change requests flow to the director instead of directly overwriting protected records.

Billing and Documents

  • Balances, invoices, payment placeholders, receipts, tuition policy notes, processing recovery language, and required documents.
  • Parent sees only own-family billing, documents, messages, children, and notification settings.

Preferences

  • Email, SMS, push, and portal notification preferences when channels are enabled by the school or tenant.
  • Language, delivery, reminder, and digest preferences are scoped to the parent user.

System Rules To Preserve

  • Every record stays tenant-scoped and school-scoped before role permissions are evaluated.
  • Location ID format remains "ST | City" so website inquiries sort to the correct CRM school profile.
  • School onboarding captures local rates, classrooms, subsidy rules, balance rules, invoice rules, compliance preferences, and notification defaults.
  • Parent and teacher views are limited to assigned family or classroom context. Sensitive child, custody, medical, billing, and incident data needs explicit access.
  • AI output is draft-only for safety, medical, custody, legal, billing, and compliance-sensitive workflows.
  • Live changes should remain rollback-friendly with audit logs, exports, and last-known-healthy deployment awareness.
